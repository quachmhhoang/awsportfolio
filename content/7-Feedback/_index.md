### INTERNSHIP EVALUATION REPORT AT AWS VIETNAM
**Company:** Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited (AWS Vietnam)  
**Internship position:** Cloud Engineer  
**Internship period:** [(From 17/04/2026 - 10/07/2026)]  
**Mentor/Manager:** Nguyen Gia Hung

---

### 1. Overview of the Internship Organization
Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited is the official branch of AWS - the world's most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud computing platform.
* **Work environment:** Strongly reflects Amazon's global culture with the "Day 1" mindset, which treats every day as the first day of a startup, and is guided by the well-known **16 Leadership Principles**.
* **Office:** Modern and professional, providing strong support for a creative and flexible working space through hybrid working.
* **Team:** Consists of leading experts and solution engineers in the cloud computing industry who are always ready to support and share knowledge, following the Peculiar and Day 1 culture.

---

### 2. Internship Objectives
* **Technical Knowledge:** Gain deeper exposure to AWS core services, including Compute, Storage, Database, Security, and Networking, as well as modern technology trends such as *Serverless, AI/ML (Amazon Bedrock), and DevOps*.
* **Professional Skills:** Learn how to analyze real business problems and design standard system architectures based on the *AWS Well-Architected Framework*.
* **Soft Skills:** Practice critical thinking, English communication in a multinational environment, solution presentation skills for customers, and effective teamwork.

---

### 3. Work Content & Projects Participated In
Throughout the internship, I was trusted with practical tasks and projects, including:
* **Research & Internal Training:** Completed the learning path and achieved AWS professional certifications, such as *AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner* or *AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate*.
* **Participation in a Real Project:** Designed and built a Proof of Concept (PoC) for the automated request management system **"AWS Helpdesk Portal"**, applying a Serverless architecture using API Gateway, Lambda, DynamoDB, SQS, and SES, combined with a CI/CD automation flow to optimize operations.
* **Support for the Solutions Architecture Team (SA Team):** Prepared technical documents and researched case studies of large customers in the Vietnamese market to identify solutions for cost optimization and improved security.

---

### 4. Self-Evaluation of Achieved Results

### 📈 Strengths and Positive Results
* **Technical Growth:** Gained a solid understanding of designing systems with good scalability, high availability, and static/dynamic security on the cloud.
* **Absorbing Corporate Culture:** Learned how to apply core principles such as *Customer Obsession* and *Bias for Action* to daily problem-solving.
* **Excellent Completion of Personal/Group Project:** The Serverless demo project ran stably, optimized cost at around ~$26/month for 50,000 requests, and received high evaluation from the AWS Mentor Council.

### 🔍 Areas for Improvement
* **Deep Technical Optimization Skills:** Need more time to further develop advanced troubleshooting skills for large and complex distributed systems.
* **Commercial Presentation Skills:** Explaining complex technical concepts in simple business language for C-level audiences is still a small challenge that needs further practice.

---

### 5. Evaluation of Environment & Mentor
* **Mentor and Team:** The mentor had extremely strong expertise, closely followed weekly progress, and not only provided technical direction but also guided a clear career development path. The engineering team at AWS Vietnam was very open, with no distance between interns and seniors.
* **Development Opportunities:** AWS provides unlimited learning resources, including AWS Skill Builder, Qwiklabs, and AWS Credits for hands-on practice. This is an ideal launchpad for any student who wants to pursue cloud computing.

---

### 6. Conclusion
The internship at **AWS Vietnam** was an invaluable experience that helped me narrow the large gap between academic theory in the classroom and the demanding, professional reality of the global IT industry. I have gained enough confidence, knowledge, and skills to be ready to enter the official labor market.

**Aspiration:** After graduation, I hope to have the opportunity to continue contributing and apply for full-time positions at AWS Vietnam through programs such as *AWS Tech U* or by directly applying for solution engineer positions, bringing cloud technology to support the development of Vietnamese businesses.
